# Setup Sahla 90-day operating plan

This calendar starts on the verified publication date. It converts the launch package into a repeatable evidence, content, distribution, measurement, and affiliate routine. Activities are ordered by dependency; performance numbers are not forecasts.

## Operating principles

- Diagnose before recommending; compatibility and skip guidance precede commercial CTAs.
- Treat price, stock, seller, fulfillment, and affiliate state as dated observations.
- Keep Egypt claims Egypt-specific. MENA expansion waits for new retail, search, program, and language research.
- Change canonical facts at the earliest responsible gate, then rebuild downstream artifacts.
- Use original visual assets. Retailer pages remain evidence sources unless current permission says otherwise.
- Label operating goals `TARGET`; record actual results separately as observations.

## Recurring operating rhythm

### Weekly

- Review Search Console/approved analytics for collection failures, query-to-page mismatches, and high-friction journeys.
- Triage reader questions and corrections; separate a content idea from an evidence-backed claim.
- Review commercial-page disclosures after any editorial or component change.
- Record one prioritized action, owner, evidence, expected learning, and due date.

### Launch day plus every 14 days

- Check exact model/variant, direct and affiliate destinations, dated price, stock signal, seller/fulfillment, return/warranty wording used in copy, image-rights state, and reviewer time.
- Recheck Havit F2069 and JOYROOM JR-ZS368 first because the research identifies tighter refresh risk, then complete the full catalog.
- If a failed link or model mismatch appears, remove the prominent CTA immediately and use `LINK DISABLED — REVIEW REQUIRED`.

### Monthly

- Close a measurement period only if tracking was stable; otherwise label it incomplete.
- Review channel effort against useful visits and retailer outbound actions, not vanity reach alone.
- Decide which one or two content improvements receive focus; avoid changing multiple variables without a record.
- Review affiliate applications, territory questions, account policy changes, and undisclosed owner dependencies.

### Every 90 days

- Reassess search intent, qualitative demand evidence, technical claims, compatibility, internal links, article usefulness, disclosure, program terms, and continued fit of each recommendation.
- Repeat all downstream checks for any changed evidence, claim, provider, product, asset, or article.
- Archive a dated refresh record with reviewer, sources, changes, and rebuilt artifacts.

### Immediate failed link/correction response

1. Remove the failed link from prominent CTAs.
2. Set `LINK DISABLED — REVIEW REQUIRED` and record the observed failure.
3. Restore the last known-safe direct destination only after exact model/market verification.
4. Reopen the earliest affected evidence or claim gate.
5. Rebuild, retest, and record the correction before republishing.
